PhD Position Open

PhD Studentship in Building & Urban Energy, AI & Social Equity

We are seeking highly motivated PhD candidates interested in developing AI-driven solutions for building and urban energy modeling, climate change adaptation, and social equity.

Research Areas

  • Building & urban energy simulation and optimization
  • Climate adaptation and resilience
  • AI methods (CV, LLMs, multi-agent systems)
  • Socioeconomic dynamics

Requirements

  • Master's degree (or exceptional Bachelor's) in Engineering, Computer Science, Social Sciences, or related fields
  • Strong initiative and persistence in solving challenging problems
  • Strong programming skills and knowledge of AI
  • Excellent English communication skills

About PhD Studies at HKU

The University of Hong Kong (HKU) is a leading research university in Asia. PhD students receive competitive scholarships and have access to world-class research facilities.

PhD students may be supported by the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS), the HKU Presidential PhD Scholar Programme (HKU-PS), or the HKU Postgraduate Scholarship (PGS), the general funding scheme available to all admitted PhD students.
