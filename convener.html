Convener

I am an Incoming Assistant Professor at the Division of Real Estate and Construction, HKU.

Previously, I was an Eric and Wendy Schmidt AI in Science Postdoctoral Fellow at the National University of Singapore and a Visiting Scholar at MIT. My research focuses on urban building energy modelling, climate change adaptation, and building decarbonization, leveraging AI, digital twins, and physics-based methods.

I received my PhD in the Built Environment from NUS (2024), MSc in Smart Buildings and Digital Engineering from UCL (2019, Distinction), and BEng in Civil Engineering from University of Bath (2018, First-class honours).
